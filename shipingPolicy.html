Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery time of 20 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Estimated delivery time of 2-3 business days.

2. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

3. Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on the total weight of the order and the selected shipping method. The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process.

4. International Shipping

We currently do not offer international shipping. We only ship within the United States.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order using the provided tracking number on our website or the carrier's website.

6. Lost or Damaged Packages

In the event that your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer support team within 7 days of the estimated delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

7. Returns and Refunds

For information about our returns and refunds policy, please refer to our Returns and Refunds Policy page.
